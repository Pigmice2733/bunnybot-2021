XBOX CONTROLLER 0 (driver)
right trigger RT (axis) - move forwards
left trigger LT (axis) - move backwards
right stick (x-axis) - turn fast
left stick (x-axis) - turn slow
A button (hold while RT/LT) - half speed
B button (hold while RT/LT) - max motor speed
right bumper RB (press) - turn right 90 degrees
left bumper LB (press) - turn left 90 degrees

XBOX CONTROLLER 1 (operator)
A button AND right bumper RB (press at the same time) - open dispenser
B button (press) - close dispenser
X button (press) - initialize Intake and ColorSorter
